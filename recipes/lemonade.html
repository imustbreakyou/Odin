Lemonade

Home

Description

Lemonade is a tasty summer drink that is renowned globally. This recipe harkens back to my youth at the lemonade stand.

Ingredients

  • Juice of 10 Lemons
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 6 Cups Water

Steps

  1. Juice Lemons
  2. Mix Ingredients
  3. Add water, ice, serve and enjoy!
