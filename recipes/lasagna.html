Description

Lasagna is a tasty dish from Italy, but known widely in the United States as the go to meal for many households. This recipe is from my Nona and I hope you love it!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • 2 pounds beef
  • 1 lb mozzeralla chees
  • 1 lb ricotta chees

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta
  2. Cook Meat
  3. Layer Meat, Pasta, Chese until dish is filled
  4. Bake at 425 f for 45 minutes
  5. Let cool for 15 minutes, serve and enjoy!
