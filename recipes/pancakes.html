Pancakes are a global phenomenon taking the globe by a phenominal storm! Rich and fluffy, tasty and yummy with protien?! YEs these kodiak pancakes will feed you and help you feel better about your food!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Kodiak Pancake Mix
  • 3/4 Cup of Water
  • 1 egg (optional)

Steps

  1. Wisk ingredients together in a bowl
  2. Butter pan over medium low heat
  3. Pour batter onto pan
  4. Flip when bubbles begin
  5. Remove after 2 more minutes
  6. Enjoy!
